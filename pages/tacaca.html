Lets make a tacacá

Ingredients

  • 1 liter of tucupi (cassava broth)
  • 1 bunch of jambu
  • 200g of driep shrimp
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • Hot peppers to taste
  • Salt

Preparation

  1. Take the tucupi to the boil. Like, seriously, get that liquid gold bubbling
  2. Add the jambu, which is basically the Brazilian version of popping candy, but with a side of mild anesthesia
  3. In a separate pan, throw some garlic into a fiesta of olive oil, mix in the dried shrimp, hot peppers, and a sprinkle of rebellion—season with salt.
  4. Merge this madness into the tucupi-jambu concoction.
  5. Serve it hot. Like your google history
