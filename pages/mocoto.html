Its mocotó time

Ingredients

  • 1 kg of mocotó (cow foot)
  • 1 big chopped onion
  • 3 cloves of garlic, because why not
  • 2 chopped tomatoes
  • Greenery (cheiro-verde) for the Instagram photo
  • Salt and pepper for the drama

Preparation

  1. Boil the mocotó until it starts questioning its life choices.
  2. In a pan, sauté the onion, garlic, and tomatoes like they just betrayed you.
  3. Add the mocotó to the party.
  4. Sprinkle greenery, salt, and pepper as if seasoning a Shakespearean tragedy.
  5. Cook for a bit, serve it like you're presenting evidence in a court drama
