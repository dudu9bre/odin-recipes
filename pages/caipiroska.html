Hiiiii caipiroska

  • 1 lemon, the life give lemons etc
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar, because life is bittersweet
  • Ice, because you're chilling through it all
  • 50 ml of vodka cause's vodka
  1. Cut the lemon into pieces, like your ex chopped your heart. (spoiler: you forget that you alive when the drink is over)
  2. Add sugar and crush the lemon like you're smashing the patriarchy.
  3. Throw in the ice, because you're colder than Elsa.
  4. Pour vodka like you're pouring out your feelings.
  5. Stir it like you're stirring up trouble.
  6. Decorate with a lemon slice, because life needs a garnish
  7. Serve and enjoy your caipiroska, the cocktail version of your unpredictable emotions.
